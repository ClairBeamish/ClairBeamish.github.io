Water Polo: On the Moon

Water Polo: On the Moon or WPOTM is one of our most popular games to play in our park. It is what it says on the can; it is like water polo where the person who is it says Marco and someone says polo and the person it tries to tag them, but for Water Polo: On the Moon it is 3D with your movements and the person who is it says --- and the people not it say ---. Before playing this game you must sign the wavor they provide at the ride.

Space Shark

This isn't so much a ride, but this is our friendly space shark. we asked our selfs what would it be like if we got a shark and placed it in a ball full of water and put it on the moon. The outcome was better than we expected, we have a new game where the shark chases you around the moon and if you escape to the end of the course without getting caught you get to pick one of our many alien pets! We also ask if you sign a wavor before particapating in this game.

Rollercoaster Of Zero Gravity

Our final mention of rides/activites is Rollercoaster of Zero Gravity. we shoot you up 50 feet from the moon then you slowly move to the edge of the top of the rollercoaster. Once you reach the top you slowly go back down the rest of the way! Amazing for thrill seekers and people who wanna live on the edge and have a little table spoon of danger.
